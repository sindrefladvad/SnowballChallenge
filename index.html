Solution

Reduce, Reuse and Recycle.

By storing cans of CO2 we manage to capture immense amounts of possible pollution. Solving the climate crisis is not an easy task. We belive that by storing CO2 we can help the world. Our Carbon Capture cans are made of 100% recycled aluminum. They are also recyclable so your can will live forever in a circular life of infinite production. CO2 is captured from our high tech state of the art capturing facilities.

We capture the excess production emition and pack it straight back in the can. This way our production is a null sum game. Producing one can yields 170g CO2. In one can we can store 1700g of CO2. Small can, large impact.
